In July 2024, I visited my family in Shanghai. It was a nostalgic and heartwarming experience to be back in the bustling city, surrounded by familiar sights, sounds, and, of course, the amazing food. Here are some highlights from my trip.

Shanghai has changed a lot over the years, but it retains its unique blend of tradition and modernity. Walking through the historic streets, I was reminded of my childhood, yet amazed by the city's constant growth and transformation.

No trip to Shanghai is complete without indulging in the local cuisine. From street food to fine dining, every meal was a memorable experience. The flavors of Shanghai bring back so many memories, especially when shared with family.

Overall, my trip to Shanghai was a heartwarming experience, filled with laughter, food, and family. It reminded me of the importance of staying connected to one's roots, no matter how far life takes you.
